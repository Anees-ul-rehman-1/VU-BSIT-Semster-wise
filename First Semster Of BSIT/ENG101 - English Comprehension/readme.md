# 📚 Course Overview

ENG101 focuses on improving English reading, writing, grammar, comprehension, and communication skills. The course builds strong academic and professional English foundations.

---

# 📖 Lecture Breakdown (45 Lectures)

## 📘 Reading Skills (Lectures 1–21)
- Reading & Dictionary Skills  
- Pronunciation  
- Main Idea & Topic Identification  
- References & Context  
- Scanning & Skimming  
- Vocabulary (Synonyms, Antonyms, Word Formation)  
- Inference & Interpretation  
- Fact vs Opinion  
- Tone & Intention  
- Graphical Information (Charts, Flowcharts)  

---

## ✍️ Writing Skills (Lectures 22–41)
- Sentence Structure & Types  
- Unity, Coherence & Emphasis  
- Sentence Error Identification & Correction  
- Subject-Verb Agreement  
- Pronouns & References  
- Word Choice  
- Punctuation (Comma, Apostrophe)  
- Paragraph Writing  
- Essay Writing & Outlines  
- Paraphrasing & Summary Writing  
- Text Organization Techniques  

---

## 📘 Grammar & Language Skills (Lectures 42–45)
- Tenses & Passive Voice  
- Adverbs & Word Order  
- Articles Usage  
- Language Functions  

---

# 📝 Assessment Breakdown

- Quizzes (Multiple)  
- Mid-Term Exam  
- GDB (Graded Discussion Board)  
- Final Term Exam

# 🎯 Learning Outcomes

After completing ENG101, students will be able to:
- Read and understand academic texts  
- Write structured paragraphs and essays  
- Apply correct grammar rules  
- Improve vocabulary and communication skills
