# 📚 Course Overview

MTH101 focuses on calculus fundamentals including limits, derivatives, integrals, applications of differentiation, sequences, series, and analytical geometry concepts.

This course builds strong mathematical reasoning and problem-solving skills required in engineering, computer science, and data-related fields.

## 📘 Unit 1: Basics & Functions (Lectures 1–8)
- Real Numbers, Inequalities  
- Absolute Value  
- Coordinate Plane & Graphs  
- Lines & Circles  
- Functions & Operations  

---

## 📗 Unit 2: Limits & Continuity (Lectures 9–13)

- Computational & Rigorous Limits  
- Continuity

- Tangent Lines & Rates of Change  
- Derivatives  
- Chain Rule  
- Implicit Differentiation  
- Applications of Derivatives  
- Maxima & Minima  
- Mean Value Theorem & Rolle’s Theorem

## 📘 Unit 4: Integration (Lectures 25–39)

- Substitution Method  
- Definite Integrals

- Area Between Curves

- Improper Integrals

## 📊 Unit 5: Sequences & Series (Lectures 40–45)
