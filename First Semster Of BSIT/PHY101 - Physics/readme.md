# 📚 Course Overview

PHY101 covers fundamental and advanced physics concepts including mechanics, thermodynamics, waves, electromagnetism, optics, relativity, and modern physics.

This course builds strong analytical and problem-solving skills required in science and engineering fields.

# 🎯 Learning Outcomes

After completing PHY101, students will be able to:
- Understand classical mechanics  
- Analyze electric and magnetic fields  
- Understand wave and optical phenomena  
- Learn basics of relativity and quantum physics  
- Apply physics in real-world systems
